Building E

The instructions on building E are found by navigating from

    http://www.erights.org/download/index.html

First, follow the link for your version of E.  Then, follow the link
for building on your platform.  For example, if your E version is
0.8.13x, and your platform is windows, you should find yourself at

    http://www.erights.org/download/0-8-13/windows-src.html
